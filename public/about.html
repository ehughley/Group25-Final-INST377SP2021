There are a number of sources on the internet where users can find information on war related facts. However, there is a lack of organized data regarding war statistics across the globe. So, we decided to tackle this information gap by creating a timeline of global war statistics from years 1823-2003.

The timeline is organized in chronological order. Users can search for a specific country and receive filtered results- a timeline of all the wars that country has participated in, organized in chronological order.
